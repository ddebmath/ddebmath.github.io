If we look closely at the graph, then we can see that the red secant line “approaches” the blue tangent line at the point \(x=0\text{.}\) This happens as the length of the interval between the two points (where the left end point is fixed at 0 and the right end point approaches 0) gets smaller and smaller. This can be verified by looking at the black and green secant lines which are closer to the blue tangent line than the original red secant line.
